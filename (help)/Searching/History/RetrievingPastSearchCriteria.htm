Retrieving past search criteria

The Search form (from Step 1. Specifying criteria) contains a Load... button (shown circled in red Figure 1).

Figure 1. Search form (with load button circled)

To access your Search history: click on the Load button. This will launch the Search history form (shown in Figure 2).

Figure 2. Search history form

Selecting

To select criteria from any previous search simply: click on the row.

Note: selecting a row is selecting all of the criteria for a future search. Presently, there is no way to select only one criterion.

Note: if you need more space to view: the form itself is resizable and scrollbars are also provided. Additionally, the table headings are also resizable.

Okaying or Cancelling

When you have finished making a selection, click on the Okay button and the Search history form will close and the Search form you return to will be populated with the criteria of the past search that you selected.

Alternatively, if you would like to cancel: click on the Cancel button and the Search history form will close and the Search form you return to will remain unchanged.

In either case: see Searching, Step 1. Specifying criteria to continue on a search.

Note: if you did not load the correct past search criteria that you were looking for simply: click on the Search form Load button again (Figure 1), select a different item, and click on the Okay button again (Figure 2). This will not affect your Search history in any way.

Sorting

By default, the files are sorted in chronological order based on when you search for them. To sort the files based on Type, Pattern, Options, XPath, Filter, and Paths: click on the respective table heading.

To alternate between ascending and descending order: click again on the already selected table heading. This will toggle from ascending to descending or from descending to ascending based on the current state.
