Expressions

Searches can be made with three types of 'term identifying' expression, see:

  • Simple expressions
  • Boolean expressions
  • Regular expressions

Additionally, when searching through EPUB documents, an XPath expression can also be used as a 'location identifying' expression, see:

  • XPath expressions

An XPath expression can be combined with any of the above 'term identifying' expressions.

Note: an XPath expression will only be used when searching through EPUB documents. It will be ignored when searching through Word and PDF documents.
