Step 3. Viewing individual results

After reviewing the list of results in Step 2. You can then view an individual result in an external viewer (as shown in Figure 1).

Figure 1. Main form with Results View and Calibre open to result

In the above example Calibre is open to the section "4 Security concepts" of the "Web security and encryption" EPUB. By clicking on any of the blue underlined hyperlinks listed in the Results view, the document will open in your preferred external viewer.

Viewers

External viewers will need to be configured in order for documents to launch in this way. See: Viewers.

Closing results tab

Once you have finished viewing all of the results you wanted to see. To close the Results tab: select the Tab > Close option from the menu, as shown in Figure 2 below.

Figure 2. Main form with Results View and Tab > Close menu item highlighted

Beginning a new search

To begin a new search, start again at: Step 1. The criteria for the search you have just conducted, has been saved in your Search history, see: History.
