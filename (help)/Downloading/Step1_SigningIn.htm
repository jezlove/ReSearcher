Step 1. Signing-in

Naturally, the first step in the download process is authentication and the window pictured below in Figure 1 is for collecting your credentials. Enter your username and password into the required fields.

Figure 1. Sign-in form

Username and password

Note: your username could be your email address (that you first used to sign-up with the Open University), your OUCU, or your PI.

Note: if you should need to reset your password, you will need to do this through the actual Open University website:
https://msds.open.ac.uk/signon/SAMSDefault/SAMSReset1_Default.aspx?URL=http://www.open.ac.uk/

Remembering your credentials

If you would like ReSearcher to remember your sign-in details: check the Remember me checkbox.

Note: your password when saved to disk will be done so securely with the use of encryption.

Signing-in

When you have finished entering your username and password, click on the Sign in button.

Note: communication with the OU server is conducted securely over the HTTPS protocol.

If authentication is successful then this window will close and you will proceed to Step 2.

Cancelling

If you would like to cancel: click on the Cancel button. This will abandon the download process and close this window.
