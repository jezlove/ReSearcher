Step 2. Checking for downloads

After Step 1 has completed. The program will begin scanning the OU website for available downloads.

Note: this may take some time.

Since progress is hard to gauge at this step, a 'marquee' progress bar continually updates. Additionally, any update messages are displayed in a 'running log'. These are both pictured below in Figure 2.

Figure 1. Checking for downloads form

Once the program has finished gathering a list of new content, it is compared against the items in your library.

Cancelling

If you would like to cancel: click on the Stop button. This will abandon the download process (your user profile will be signed-out) and you will have to start again from Step 1.
