Step 4. Waiting for files to download

Upon Step 3 having been completed. The program will begin downloading all of the available downloads that you selected. Progress is recorded as a percentage and also displayed with a progress bar (pictured below in Figure 1). Additionally, the file that is currently being downloaded is displayed as part of a 'running log'.

Figure 1. Downloading form

Stopping

To stop the progress of downloading: click on the Stop button. This will abandon the downloading procedure (your user profile will be signed-out) and you will have to start again from Step 1 to download any additional files.

Note: files that completed downloading are not deleted. This is why this operation is called stopping rather than cancelling.
